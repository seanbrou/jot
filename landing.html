Features
Built for people who hate organizing notes

Every feature in Jot is designed to reduce friction between capturing a thought and finding it later.

⚡

AI Auto-Filing

Jot reads your note and files it in the right notebook automatically. No manual sorting, no folder trees.

📋

Kanban Board View

Drag and drop notes between columns. Move them to archive or a different notebook in one swipe.

📅

Calendar View

See your notes on a month or week calendar. Timed notes shown as scheduled blocks, everything else as all-day events.

🔍

Instant Search

Find any note in milliseconds. Search by keyword, status, or notebook — results update as you type.

📓

Smart Notebooks

Create custom notebooks with descriptions. Jot uses those descriptions to file notes accurately.

☁️

Cloud Sync

Your notes are synced in real-time across every device. Sign in with Google and pick up anywhere.

FAQ
Common questions
How does AI filing work?
When you create a note, Jot analyzes the content using AI and compares it against your notebook descriptions. It then files the note in the most relevant notebook. You can always move it manually if needed.
Can I try Jot before paying?
Yes — sign up with Google and start using Jot right away. We'll let you explore all the features before you decide.
Is my data secure?
Your notes are stored securely on Convex infrastructure. We use Google OAuth for authentication and never access your note content for any purpose other than filing.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Absolutely. Cancel anytime and you'll keep access until the end of your billing period. No questions asked, no hoops to jump through.
